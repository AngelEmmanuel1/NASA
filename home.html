DEAP Institute

The case study the proposed DEAP Institute will ML-based approach in tracking and projecting impacts of combined effects of natural and anthropogenic disturbances (storm-related flooding and sea level rise) on the coastal the community and resources.

The primary research setting/test areas for the B-CU and ERAU teams will be the Indian River Lagoon (IRL) and northern coastal region on the Florida Atlantic Ocean.
